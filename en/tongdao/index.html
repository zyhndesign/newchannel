Project Background

Dong Autonomous County of Tongdao is located in the junction of Hunan, Guangxi, Guizhou provinces. The population of Tongdao is 225 thousand, which 78.4% of the population is Dong. The extremely beautiful ecological environment and the well preserved national culture make here "Dong cultural mecca". It is also a blessed place that displays the charm of Dong, the brilliance of culture and the harmony of social. Even though the county has multiple advantages like rich products, forested land (74%) and an annual output of 170 thousand of cubic meters of commercial timber, the single economy form became the barrier that still kept it a national poverty county which the average net income of the farmers was only 1571 yuan in 2008. This project is targeting at minority nationality region like Tongdao, which has the conditions like unique natural environment and rich intangible cultural heritage, but undeveloped in economy. We combine the strength of different professions, establishing an International Design Innovation Alliance and an Network-based Information Platform and promoting the cultural consciousness and industrial innovation of the local residents participatively by schemes such as proper town planning, eco-tourism development, information and logistics network construction, integration of products and services system, conservation of intangible cultural heritage etc. Considering keeping both the harmonious life of residents and the need of development, we make the county jump to an innovation community centered with culture and information from an undeveloped Farming civilization community without experiencing the phase of resource consumption-based industrial development, then become a model of a new-type industrialization and two-oriented society.
